Transforming Wastewater with the Power of Zinc

The Basics

Viprobac is a mineral-based formulation engineered specifically for wastewater treatment. By harnessing the natural antimicrobial properties of zinc, together with a proprietary blend of essential minerals.

Pathogen Reduction

Zinc ions disrupt bacterial cell membranes and promote the coagulation of organic matter. This diminishes harmful microorganisms and pathogens while precipitating suspended solids.

Non-Chemical

Unlike conventional methods that depend on synthetic chemicals, Viprobac's mineral interaction works in harmony with nature. It minimizes the formation of toxic byproducts and sludge.

Process Efficiency

The ion-exchange reactions driven by zinc help maintain a balanced mineral profile in the water. This means lower maintenance costs and fewer operational challenges.

Research Backed

Recent studies into mineral‐based wastewater management have shown that incorporating trace elements like zinc not only curbs microbial proliferation but also supports long‐term water stability.

Resource Recovery

Viprobac leverages research‐backed benefits to turn wastewater into a safe and reusable resource, creating a sustainable cycle for water management.

Boosting Agricultural Growth with Essential Minerals

The Basics

Once wastewater is safely treated, it can be repurposed for agricultural applications. This is where Viprogro comes into play as a mineral growth enhancer.

Soil Enrichment

Viprogro provides a bioavailable mix of zinc along with other essential minerals. This mineral cocktail replenishes soils that may be depleted of nutrients.

Enhanced Growth

By boosting enzymatic activities and photosynthesis through a more balanced mineral environment, Viprogro can improve nutrient uptake in plants.

Water Synergy

When used with Viprobac-treated wastewater, Viprogro helps create a circular system. The reclaimed water acts as an ideal medium for nutrient uptake.

Yield Improvement

The result is increased crop productivity and improved quality of the harvest, leading to better returns on investment for farmers.

Research Based

Research into sustainable irrigation has highlighted the advantages of using sophisticated mineral formulations to recover and enhance water resources.
